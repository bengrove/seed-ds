# Homepage

## Purpose
Top-of-funnel landing for prospects and returning members. Positions Seed as whole-body-health-starts-in-the-gut, leads with DS-01® (flagship), surfaces product line expansion (DM-02, AM-02, PM-02), and routes prospects to the Biotics Quiz.

## Structure
1. **Hero** — DS-01® Daily Synbiotic, headline "A healthy gut can change your life", dual CTA (Shop DS-01 / Take the Quiz)
2. **Product line module** — "Whole body health starts in the gut" — 4-up product grid (DS-01, DM-02, AM-02, PM-02) with badges (Bestseller, New)
3. **Bundle callout** — Daily Essentials Duo (DS-01 + DM-02), "Save 25%"
4. **ViaCap® technology module** — capsule-in-capsule explainer with outer/inner capsule breakdown and stat ("↑0x Lactobacillus spp.")
5. **"You are more than human" module** — microbiome education entry point → Microbiome 101
6. **Social proof** — "Over 1 million health transformations"
7. **Press/editorial** — stories from scientists, innovators, members (Fast Company, Forbes links)
8. **SeedLabs module** — "Because health is not just human" — brand/mission extension
9. **Bottom CTA** — "Change your gut health for good" → Shop Now

## Key patterns
- **Layout:** Full-bleed hero, centered max-width sections below, alternating background treatments between modules.
- **Responsive:** 4-up product grid likely collapses to 2-up on tablet, 1-up stack on mobile. ViaCap visual likely reflows from side-by-side to stacked.
- **Interactive:** Hero CTAs are primary routes; product cards link to PDPs; quiz entry point appears twice (hero + likely in nav).
- **Compliance:** Asterisks (*) on structure/function claims, degree symbol (°) on strain-specific claims — see `copy/compliance.md`.

## Component usage
- Hero with dual CTA
- Product card (4-up grid, badge variants: Bestseller, New)
- Bundle card with percentage-off treatment
- Stat callout with up-arrow indicator
- Editorial/press link cards
- Mission/brand extension module (SeedLabs pattern)
