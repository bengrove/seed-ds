# Vaginal Synbiotic (VS-01™)

Probiotic suppository for the vaginal microbiome — three proprietary L. crispatus strains in a tablet, applicator-delivered. Currently waitlist-only ("Join the Waitlist," not "Start Now"). The first product in the line for vaginal-microbiome support; the most regulatory-sensitive copy scope. HSA/FSA eligible — important commerce signal.

## Visual identity

- **Primary color:** `color.primary.seed-green` (`#1c3a13`) — brand primary. No `--color--vs` token in live CSS.
- **Section accents:** Soft mint `#dae6e5` and pale dust `#d2d8d0` used as background washes behind benefit modules. Read as clinical / clean / non-floral — explicitly avoiding the conventional "feminine wellness" pastel-pink language.
- **Secondary color:** Warm off-white / parchment (`#fcfcf7`).
- **Accent:** `color.primary.seed-green` for CTAs.
- **Badge treatment:** No "Bestseller" / "New" chip in the hero. CTA is "Join the Waitlist" rather than "Start Now" — visual signal that this isn't a steady-state retail SKU yet.
- **Typography variant:** No deviation from default Seed Sans hierarchy.

## Content specifics

- **Tagline:** "The first vaginal probiotic suppository clinically validated to establish an optimal vaginal microbiome dominated by L. crispatus."
- **Voice tone:** Notably more clinical / inclusive / educational than the rest of the line. Explicit Eidos LGBTQ+ Health Initiative partnership note ("the definition of women's health is evolving") sets the inclusivity bar.
- **Elevated claim language:**
  - "Establish an optimal vaginal microbiome dominated by L. crispatus"
  - "Three proprietary L. crispatus strains: LUCA103™, LUCA011™, LUCA009™"
  - "Tested for biocompatibility with the vaginal environment, 14 classes of allergens, 500+ contaminants, and osmolality"
  - "VS-01™ represents a tectonic shift in women's health" (provider testimonial — Erica Chidi)
  - "A paradigm shift in how we think about and care for vaginal health" (provider testimonial — Dr. Jane van Dis)
- **Simple claim language:**
  - "Day-to-day life can disrupt the balance of your vaginal microbiome, depleting its critical super defender — Lactobacillus crispatus"
  - "850mg per tablet"
  - "No refrigeration required"
  - "The two-tablet monthly protocol that maintains stability and protects against pH disruptors"
- **Disruptor list (verbatim):** Menstruation, Diet, Stress, Past Pregnancy, Exercise, Swimming, Sex, Sex Toys, Condom Use, Oral Sex, Gels and Lubricants, Certain Contraceptives, Certain Prescriptions, Certain Cleansers, Feminine Hygiene Products. Pulled from the page; quote as a list, never edit.
- **Compliance-approved phrases:**
  - "Establishes an optimal vaginal microbiome" (RCT-backed)
  - "Maintains stability against pH disruptors" (structure-function, scoped)
  - "Non-irritating, non-toxic" (regulatory testing claims)
  - Avoid: "treats BV," "prevents UTI," "cures yeast infection," any disease state. Avoid emotional / lifestyle hooks ("feel fresh," "be confident") — VS-01 voice stays clinical.
- **Required disclaimers:**
  - "VS-01™ requires your body's natural lubrication for proper dissolution, and therefore may not be as effective among women experiencing significant vaginal dryness."
  - "Each applicator is a Class I Medical Device and cannot be recycled."
  - "We recommend abstaining from sexual activity for 12 hours after inserting a tablet."

## Imagery direction

- **Hero style:** Editorial / clinical macro of the suppository tablet against an off-white field. Notably *not* using the green hero treatment of capsule SKUs.
- **Application diagram:** Step-by-step applicator illustration — schematic, not photographic. Critical that this stays clinical rather than instructional-cartoonish.
- **Validated / Allergens / USPs imagery:** Studio-grade product photography on neutral grounds. No models. No skin. The visual restraint is part of the brand position.
- **Provider portraits:** Erica Chidi and Dr. Jane van Dis appear in editorial portraits — only product in the line that uses spokesperson-style imagery.
- **Lifestyle imagery:** Largely absent. VS-01 deliberately avoids the lifestyle-photography conventions of the category.

## Template deviations

Significant deviations from canonical PDP (`pages/pdp/`):
- **Waitlist CTA, not Start Now.** "Join the Waitlist" replaces the standard CTA pattern.
- **Provider testimonial module.** Two named expert quotes prominently placed — unique to VS-01 in the current product set.
- **Disruptor grid.** A 16-item grid of vaginal-microbiome disruptors (Menstruation, Diet, Stress, etc.) — unique pattern, doesn't appear on other PDPs.
- **2-month starter kit pricing.** First-purchase price ($99) differs from monthly thereafter ($39.99). Steady-state is a two-tablet monthly protocol — different cadence than capsule SKUs.
- **HSA / FSA eligibility callout.** Reassurance line in hero — only product in the line with this commerce signal.
- **No benefit timeline.** Replaced by a "Month 1 Establish / Month 2+ Sustain" two-step protocol diagram.

- **SciCare callout.** Email-based science-team support module — present here, less prominent on capsule PDPs.
