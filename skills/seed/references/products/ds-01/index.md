# Daily Synbiotic (DS-01®)

The flagship product. The only adult synbiotic with full-formulation clinical validation across multiple RCTs. DS-01 sets the visual and editorial template most other Seed products inherit from.

## Visual identity

- **Primary color:** `color.primary.seed-green` (`#1c3a13`) — the brand primary. DS-01 owns it directly because it's the flagship; subsequent products take a distinct color from `extended-palette.*`.
- **Secondary color:** Warm off-white / parchment for type and surface contrast against the seed-green hero.
- **Accent:** Same `color.primary.seed-green` for CTAs and section emphasis — DS-01 is the only product where primary and accent collapse.
- **Badge treatment:** "Bestseller" chip near the product name in steady-state hero. Promo variants add a code badge with strikethrough original price.
- **Typography variant:** No deviation from default Seed Sans hierarchy.

## Content specifics

- **Tagline:** "Daily probiotic + prebiotic clinically validated to relieve bloating, gas, and irregularity. Powered by 24 strains that promote whole body health.*"
- **Elevated claim language:**
  - "53.6 billion AFU broad-spectrum 24-strain synbiotic"
  - "Probiotic Inner Capsule: proprietary formulation consisting of 24 probiotic strains validated through strain-specific research and free of all 14 classes of allergens identified by the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA)"
  - "Microbiota-Accessible Polyphenolic Precursors™ (MAPP)"
- **Simple claim language:**
  - "24 strains for transformative gut benefits"
  - "Reduces bloating and gas within 1 week"
  - "Improves regularity and digestive discomfort"
  - "Supports whole body health"
- **Compliance-approved phrases:**
  - "Clinically validated" (only when referring to full-formulation RCTs)
  - "Tested in multiple full formulation randomized, placebo-controlled clinical trials"
  - "Promotes whole body health"
  - Avoid: "treats," "cures," "prevents," "improves digestion" (disease claim — reroute to "relieves bloating, gas, and irregularity")

## Imagery direction

- **Hero style:** Macro / product-on-pedestal of the dual-capsule (outer pomegranate prebiotic + inner probiotic) against a duck-green field. Capsule transparency reads as the visual signature.
- **Ingredient / science imagery:** ViaCap® cross-section diagrams (clinical, blueprint-like — not photographic). SHIME® / GI-tract illustrations stay schematic, never anatomical-photograph.
- **Lifestyle imagery:** Refillable glass jar + travel vial as the steady-state lifestyle anchor — "Sustainably delivered, monthly." Lifestyle shots lean editorial / still-life, not in-use.

None — DS-01 is the canonical PDP. See `pages/pdp/` for the structural reference. Any other product PDP that diverges from this layout should document the delta in its own product file.
