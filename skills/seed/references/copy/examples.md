### Retention (post-purchase)

**Onboarding welcome**
- Headline: `Welcome to Seed.`
- Subcopy: `Your first delivery arrives in 2 business days. Take 2 capsules daily on an empty stomach, first thing in the morning.`

**Shipment confirmation**
- Subject: `Your DS-01® is on its way.`
- Body open: `We shipped your order. Track it anytime from your account.`

**Pause confirmation**
- Headline: `Subscription paused`
- Body: `We'll skip your next delivery and resume automatically on [date].`
- Secondary CTAs: `Change pause date`, `Resume now`

**Cancel flow (pre-cancel)**
- Headline: `Before you cancel`
- Body: `Your subscription is active through [date]. You can make a change that fits better, or cancel anytime.`
- Options:
  - `Pause for a while. Take 1-2 months off. Resume automatically.`
  - `Change frequency. Move to every 2 months instead of monthly.`
- Continue link: `Continue with cancellation`

**Cancel confirmation**
- Headline: `Subscription canceled`
- Body: `You'll keep access through [date]. We kept your account details. You can restart anytime.`

**Win-back email (honest)**
- Subject: `We'd love to know what didn't work.`
- Body open: `You recently canceled your DS-01® subscription. If it was a fit issue, a scheduling issue, or something else, we'd rather learn than assume.`

## Do / Don't cheat sheet (quick reference)

### Promos
- Do: `DS-01® Now 25% Off†`
- Not: `25% Off DS-01®†`

### Benefits
- Do: `Daily DS-01® seeds your gut with beneficial bacteria.`
- Not: `When you take DS-01®, you are seeding your gut with beneficial bacteria.`

### Claims
- Do: `Relieve Bloating in 1 Week*`
- Not: `Relieve Bloating in 1 Week With DS-01®*`

### Headlines
- Do: `38 trillion reasons your current multivitamin falls short.`
- Not: `Your body and your microbiome need support.`

### CTAs
- Do: `Start My DS-01® Journey`
- Not: `Get Started` or `Explore more probiotics for you and your microbiome`

### Urgency
- Do: `30% off → ends today.`
- Not: `30% off is ending soon! Act fast!`
